## Car Sales Analysis - Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing car sales data to identify sales trends, customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and revenue growth opportunities. The analysis highlights key business drivers such as top-performing vehicle brands, high-revenue product categories, customer buying behavior, and sales performance across different time periods and regions.

The project was developed to transform raw transactional sales data into meaningful business insights that can support strategic decision-making and improve overall sales performance.

## Problem Statement

The automotive sales industry generates large volumes of transactional and customer data daily. However, without proper analysis, businesses may struggle to understand which vehicles generate the highest revenue, which regions perform best, and what factors influence customer purchasing decisions.
The company faced challenges in identifying high-performing and underperforming vehicle categories, understanding seasonal sales patterns, and optimizing sales strategies based on customer behavior and market demand. In addition, inconsistent and unprocessed raw data made it difficult to extract reliable insights for business growth and operational planning.
Without data-driven insights, important business decisions such as inventory management, pricing strategies, marketing campaigns, and sales forecasting become less effective and may lead to missed revenue opportunities.
This project addresses these challenges by applying data analytics techniques to clean, process, and analyze car sales data in order to uncover trends, identify performance gaps, and generate actionable recommendations that support business growth and improve sales performance.
## Aim of the Project
The aim of this project is to perform a comprehensive analysis of car sales data in order to identify meaningful sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, and revenue-driving factors within the automotive business environment.
The project seeks to transform raw and unstructured sales data into valuable business intelligence that can assist stakeholders in making informed decisions. Through data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and visualization, the project aims to provide a clearer understanding of how sales performance changes across different vehicle categories, regions, time periods, and customer segments.
Furthermore, the project aims to identify opportunities for improving profitability, increasing customer engagement, optimizing inventory management, and strengthening overall sales strategies. By leveraging data analytics tools and visualization techniques, the analysis supports strategic planning and enables the business to make data-driven decisions that contribute to long-term growth and operational efficiency.

## Objectives
To successfully achieve the project aim, the following steps were completed:
Conducted project planning and workflow design using Miro diagrams
Designed a Gantt chart in Canva to manage project timelines and track progress
Imported and processed the dataset using Databricks
Cleaned and transformed raw sales data for accurate analysis
Converted text-based price values into numeric formats
Created calculated fields such as:
Total Revenue
Profit Margin
Sales Performance Categories
Used SQL queries to analyze sales trends and customer purchasing behavior
Grouped sales transactions by month, quarter, and year to identify trends over time
Created pivot tables and charts in Microsoft Excel
Developed visual dashboards to communicate key insights and findings
Designed and finalized the presentation using Canva

## Summary of Results

The analysis revealed several important business insights regarding vehicle sales performance, revenue generation, and customer purchasing behavior.
Key findings included:
Certain vehicle brands and categories consistently generated the highest revenue
Sales performance varied significantly across different time periods and regions
Some products showed strong profitability while others underperformed
Seasonal trends influenced customer purchasing behavior and overall sales volume
High-margin vehicles contributed significantly to total business revenue
The project also highlighted opportunities for:
Improving inventory planning
Optimizing marketing strategies
Increasing focus on high-performing vehicle categories
Enhancing customer targeting strategies
Supporting future sales forecasting and business planning
These insights provide a strong foundation for data-driven decision-making and improved business performance within the automotive sales industry.

## Conclusion
This case study demonstrates how data analytics can be used to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights. By combining data processing, SQL analysis, visualization, and reporting techniques, the project successfully identified critical sales trends and provided strategic recommendations that can help improve revenue growth, operational efficiency, and decision-making within the automotive sales industry.
